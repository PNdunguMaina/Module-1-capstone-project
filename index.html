MASAKU 7s MUSIC CONCERT

A world-class concert set to strengthen unity among the local artists and provide a platform and opportunity for the local music enthuthiasts to have a joyous and memorable experience is happening yet again this December, in Machakos.

2022.12.16(FRI) ~17(SAT)

"Enhancing stage performance skills for our local artists"

  • Free stylingthe practice of improvising scenes when making a film or performing a play to be done by upcoming artists to the audience
  • Curtain raisera short play of one scene that will be presented as a preliminary for the main concert event
  • Live performanceThe actual event starts where all the featured artists will perform to the audience live while interacting with them
  • PhotographyThe memorable event moments will be captured by professional photographers to enhance the memory even more once the are revisited
  • Party modeEverybody will be allowed to enjoy the concert by giving room to listen and take anything that they desire and interact freely with others
